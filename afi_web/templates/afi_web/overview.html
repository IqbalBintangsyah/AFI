Our Purpose and Value

More and more people will choose green power for the sake of the planet and society. However, in order to provide a stable supply of green power, it is necessary to solve the problem of instability due to natural origin. It will be an era in which the way and quality of power transmission up to now will be questioned. Under such circumstances, afterFIT will solve the problem of instability with technology and supply electricity in order to make green power a society that everyone can use.
