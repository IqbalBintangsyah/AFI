About Us

PT afterFIT Indonesia is a leading power plant company that delivers a broad range of power plant related services such as O & M, drones, EPC, as well as other power plant related services.

Founded in Japan in 2016, afterFIT aims to make the world into a better place by making better use of various technological methods to process renewable energy.
